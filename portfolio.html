PLTW 1.1.9- The Snowglobe



Using Python's turtle feature on Visual Studies, my group and I had designed a neat snowglobe fit for a Christmas night. A snowglobe that could be gifted to loved ones, for them to stare deep into a cute and cozy home owned by two lovely snowmen. Here, they're celebrating the holidays as well, with a cute little while-loop-based wreath that mesmorizes others as the lights light up.
